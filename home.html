DISCOVER AKASHA XPLORE 2026

Akasha Xplore 2026 is the annual Astronomy Day organized by the Astronomical Society of Dharmapala Vidyalaya, Pannipitiya. The event brings together young innovators, astronomy enthusiasts, and students from across Sri Lanka to explore the wonders of space, science, and technology through exciting competitions and interactive experiences.With events including Astro Photography, Astro Quiz, Water Rocket, and Astro Presentations, Akasha Xplore 2026 aims to inspire the next generation of explorers and promote passion for astronomy and STEM education.
