Akasha Xplore 2026 is the annual Astronomy Day organized by the Astronomical Society of Dharmapala Vidyalaya, Pannipitiya, bringing together students and astronomy enthusiasts from across Sri Lanka. Through events like Astro Photography, Astro Quiz, Water Rocket, and Astro Presentations, it inspires innovation and passion for space, science, and STEM.
